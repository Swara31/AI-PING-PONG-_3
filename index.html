AI PING PONG GAME









Instructions

1. First keep your laptop screen STAIGHT.



2. Move your self approximately 3-4 FEET away from the laptop.



3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a RED dot should appear on your right wrist.



4. Now press the PLAY button, and again move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop.



5. The RED paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
